Scheduling an appointment at Stamps:

  • Allergy:
    Schedule by calling (404) 385-4995
  • Immunization:
    Schedule by calling (404) 385-4995
  • Primary Care:
    Please make an appointment on the Patient Portal
  • Psychiatry:
    Students seeking psychiatry services must first visit the Center for Assessment and Referral (CARE) for an initial assessment and referral. Appointments are not available in CARE, only walk-in service is offered during office hours of Monday through Friday from 8:00AM - 5:00PM (must be in center by 4:00PM)
  • Sports Medicine:
    Call (404) 894-1420 during business hours. When you call, press 1 to make an appointment, then press 1 again to make an appointment in the Blue, Gold, or Silver care teams
  • Travel:
    Complete the International Travel Form and fax or email the form to (404) 894-6254 or travel@health.gatech.edu
  • Women’s Health:
    All Women's Health appointments are scheduled by phone. A trained medical professional will assess your needs and assist you in making an appropriately timed appointment that works with your personal schedule and addresses your specific concerns. Same day appointments are often available for certain urgent concerns. Schedule your appointment by phone at (404) 894-1434

GaTech Covid-19 Travel Testing:

  • Covid-19 testing is available for students needing certified results for personal travel and employees needing results for business-related travel
  • Surveillance testing is not a diagnostic test and results cannot be used for travel; travel testing is done using Vault test kits
  • Cost:
    Free
  • Testing Dos and Don'ts:
    Don't register on mytest.gatech.edu prior to the test
    Don't have anything to eat or drink 30 minutes prior to testing
    Do hydrate more than 30 minutes prior to the test to allow for a large enough saliva sample
    (additional sample is required for this test)
    Do bring your cellphone to the testing site
    Do be aware of your travel requirements. Each airline requires different windows prior to testing.
    The testing site staff will not be able to advise you based on your airline’s travel needs
  • During the test:
    Notify the staff member at check-in that you need travel testing
    sand they will walk you through the process via the Vault website
  • Results:
    Results will be emailed between 48-72 hours
    The email result can be used as your travel verification
  • Locations and Hours:
    Travel testing is available at both the Price Gilbert Library and 760 Spring Street testing sites 10 a.m. – 3 p.m. when those sites are open.
    Please check Testing Locations and Hours for Winter Break and holiday hours
